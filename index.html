Frequently Asked Questions

We distribute to all the major streaming services: Spotify, Apple Music, TikTok, YouTube, Deezer, Tidal, and over 200 other streaming platforms and stores globally.
FM Digital Official offers free distribution through a process called "royalty capture." When royalties are received from Digital Service Providers, they are placed into the DeFi platform AAVE for a set period. During this time, the royalties earn interest, which we retain, while you keep 100% of the principal royalty amount. After this period, your earnings, beyond a minimal locked amount, are fully available for withdrawal. Unlike others who claim to be free, we truly are.
Transferring your catalog to FM Digital is simple. You'll need a few key pieces of information:
  • The UPC and ISRC (to help DSPs identify your existing release)
  • Release title and track title(s)
  • Artist(s) name(s)
  • The original release date
  • The exact same audio and cover art files from the previous release.
There are no upfront fees, but we offer two plans for music distribution:
  • Freemium Plan: You keep 70% of your royalties, with FM Digital retaining 30%.
  • Premium Plan: You keep 100% of your royalties, ensuring you receive every cent of your earnings.
FM Digital Official provides flexible options to suit your needs, whether you're just starting out or looking to maximize your revenue.
Yes, you can update your release after distribution. However, certain changes, like updating metadata or replacing audio files, may require additional processing time. Contact our support team for specific changes.
